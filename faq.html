FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Have Questions?

Here are some of the Frequently Asked Questions to help you get answers to.

How can I access my training?

Training is completely online. The program is available anywhere in the world. Once you are enrolled we can communicate whenever is convenient for you.

Is the training structured?

Yes, your personalised training is specifically designed to meet your needs. All of the modules have been formulated to measure your learning comprehension and aptitude. You will have the ability to respond to the learning through a variety of assessment material.

How will I know if the training is beneficial?

Each unit covers theory and practical, you will be able to test your new skills in the workplace and assess how effective you have been.

Will I have a plan that outlines the training?

Each enrolment package comes with a specific plan and report of training. The plan will outline all of the relevant details for your employer and agent when it comes time to submit your aplication.

Once I begin my training will I be able to communicate with anybody?

Yes, each student will have access to send messages directly to a learning leader. Your learning leader (LL) will always be ready to assist.

Will I obtain any qualifications from this training?

These units are not accredited, however, there are pathways in place that allow individuals to apply to have their learning assessed and accredited. These accreditations can count towards Recognition of Prior Learning for Nationally Recognised Courses and qualifications.

Can I start upskilling now?

Yes, you absolutely can start upskilling. Send us a message today and we will have you enrolled and ready to go! Welcome to the team!
